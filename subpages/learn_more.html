Siebe Houtsma

Siebe Houtsma is a local abstract artist who has shown at the Princess gallery a few times in the past. Siebe is always experimenting with a variety of materials and subject matter. This time he has utilized line, shape and colour in the works. A lot of the ideas and inspiration come from time spent in nature, mostly around the Georgian Bay areas. The gallery walls are hung with colourful canvases nicely framed in various sizes. The display runs from May 1st to June 30th. Come and have a look.
