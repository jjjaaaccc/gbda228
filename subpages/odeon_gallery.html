About

the Gallery

The Odeon Gallery is a fine little exhibition space in the basement of the Princess Twin Cinemas featuring 100 year old maple floors and 4 big walls in a 510 square foot space! The walls feature a chain hanging system with 's' hooks and is flexible for all sizes of pieces. We exhibit local artists, hosting one month and 2 month long shows. Shows are booked well in advance. The gallery is a secure place to show your work. Thousands of people pass through the cinemas each month and visit the Odeon.
